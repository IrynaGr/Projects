About me

I am a Junior Software Developer, who specialize in Web development.

I am a Web developer, currently focusing on front end development and aspiring to be a Full-Stack Developer by the end of the year. What lead me down these path is my deep love for coding. My passion for Web development started, when my friend gave me a website project to have a look at, to see what needed updating and how to go about making the new changes. This was how I got a taste for developing and I realised I wanted to learn more, so much more.

I ended up doing some aspect of Front-End Web development for a year, website. I am proficient in Html5 and CSS, Sass, adaptive design, basic of photoshop of coder, Jquery, Gulp. I have just recently finished learning JavaScript and I understand fundamental JavaScript.

You got to the site - my portfolio, which I did to show what I can do as a Front-end developer. I constantly improve my Front-end skills, actively developing as a specialist. At the moment, I work as freelancer full-time, so I'm ready for any cooperation.
