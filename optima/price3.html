Design of websites and interfaces — we offer as a separate service!

There are situations where only a good and thoughtful design of the pages is needed.

We think about the effectiveness of design. Development of the website design begins with projecting, in results of this are createted simplified graphic prototypes of all the website pages (wireframes). We consider this stage to be one of the key in creating a successful website. Basically the already developed pages structure can be viewed as a separate product.

Creative design. Search for the website's style begins with a discussion of the ideas and references of your project. Not all ideas will be applicable to your audience, not every idea is realized within the specified budget and at a reasonable time. We seek with your participation to find the best solutions in the field of design.

We remember the details. A good project is good in its little things, so we draw a lot of pages, interactive elements, different states. A small website contains from 7 to 15 different output information templates, each requires its own approach and visualization.

What in the end. We need at least 3 weeks to complete the design of the website. As a result, you get the design of all pages of the website in the format of psd. Additionally, we can make pages layout and compile a html prototype website. In doing so, you will get cross-browser layout and ready-made interactive elements on javascript or flash technology depending on the tasks and needs.
