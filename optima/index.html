Personal website

A personal website — is the site of a particular person. Typically, a website of this type is small, contains personal or biographical information or information about the services and consultations provided by that person. A personal website — is a great opportunity to express yourself, your business or your talents. Typically, a site of this type is created for a specific person interested in self-promotion, or individual entrepreneurs: artists, musicians, photographers, lawyers, doctors, creative

individuals and people of various professions.

To know what price

Main features of the personal site:

  • Exclusive and attractive design
  • The content of personal information
  • Small amount of pages, usually no more than 5-10

Reasons for creating a personal website:

  • Self-presentation
  • The opportunity to find customers, buyers, sponsors or employers
  • Convenient and direct communication with the audience
  • Quite a small price

One of the main features of a personal site — is that it provides a brief information about a person - only the most important and necessary. Therefore, its structure is limited to just a few basic sections: about yourself, services, prices, feedback form, contacts. The website of this type is great for business and creative people who need to present information about themselves in a short and attractive form to the audience.

When creating a personal website special attention is paid to how information is presented on the website. It should be in a concise way, "bright" and catchy. It is also important to carefully develop design of the website. It should be memorable, but, at the same time, unobtrusive and attractive. Exactly the design of a personal website that can advantageously play on the image and reputation of the person represented on it.

Forum website

The essence of the Forum — is to create by users (visitors to the forum) of their topics with their subsequent discussion, by posting messages within these topics. A separate topic, in fact, is a thematic guest book. Users can comment on the stated topic, ask questions about it and receive answers, as well as answer the questions of other forum users and give them advice. Inside the topic can also be arranged polls (voting). Questions and answers are saved in the forum database, and in the future can

be useful to forum participants and any Internet users who can visit the forum, knowing the link of website or getting it from search engines when searching for information.

To know what price

Subjects of forums can be the different, covering all spheres of life, and is determined either by the owners of the forum or its administration, or depends on the contingent of participants. Also forum can be specialized, for example, dedicated to music or any musical genre, as well as highly specialized — dedicated to any music group or performer, and without specialization — in this case, the users themselves choose topics that are interesting to them. Forums can exist as a independently, without connection to any website, and be a part of web portals.
